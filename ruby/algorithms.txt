- For each key on a keychain:
  - Until you can turn the doorknob, or until you have checked all the keys
      - Insert the key in the doorknob
      - Turn the key in the doorknob
      - Turn the doorknob
  - If the doorknob doesn't turn
      - Proceed to the next key
  - If you have checked  all the keys
      - Cease the operation
      - Display 'error'
